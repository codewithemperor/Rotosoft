Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our services, processes, and more below.

1. What types of solar panels do you offer?

We offer a variety of solar panels, including monocrystalline and polycrystalline options, to suit different energy needs and budgets.

2. Do you provide warranties for your inverter products?

Yes, we offer warranties for all our inverter products to ensure peace of mind for our customers. Please contact us for more details.

3. How long does a typical dish installation take?

The duration of a dish installation can vary depending on factors such as location and complexity. However, our team strives to complete installations efficiently, typically within a few hours.

4. What networking services do you offer?

We offer a range of networking services, including setup, configuration, troubleshooting, and network security enhancements, tailored to meet the needs of both residential and business clients.

5. How can I request a repair service?

To request a repair service, simply contact our customer service team via phone or email, and we will schedule a technician to assess and address the issue as soon as possible.
